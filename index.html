Mons Attack is a game within the world of monsteria where anyone can venture through and catch wild monsters. Players can catch monsters, battle them, or even breed them to create new monster. Monster caught and bred will have different attributes and stats. These attributes and stats will determine the strength of the monsters.

Features

Adventure


Explore the immersive world of monsteria in all new adventure mode!

Arena Battle


Compete with other players by battling out their monsters and climp up the leaderboard!

Breeding


Breed two monsters to create an egg and expand your collection
